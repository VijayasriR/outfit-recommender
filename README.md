## Outfit-Recommender

Pre-processing:
In male and female celebrities folders, each of the celebrities have their own folder of images. Their body proportions (chest, waist, hip and shoulder-length) are measured with respect to their height (given as input). A dataset is created, and these measurements are added.

Recommendations:
A person's image (path to image) as well their height is given as input, the body proportions are measured and compared with the celebritiesâ€™ proportions, and most similar celebrity and their Indian outfits are given as recommendations.
